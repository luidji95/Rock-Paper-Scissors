Rock-Paper-Scissors Game Instructions

  1. Game Setup:
    • You will see three pictures representing Rock, Paper, and Scissors.
    • Your goal is to select one of these pictures to make your choice.
  2. How to Play:
    • Click on the picture of your choice (Rock, Paper, or Scissors).
    • The computer will then randomly select Rock, Paper, or Scissors as well.
    • The winner of each round is determined by the following rules:
      • Rock beats Scissors
      • Scissors beats Paper
      • Paper beats Rock
    • If both you and the computer choose the same item, it's a tie and no one gets a point.
  3. Scoring:
    • Each time you win a round, you earn 1 point.
    • Each time the computer wins a round, it earns 1 point.
    • The game continues until either you or the computer reaches 3 points.
  4. Winning the Game:
    • The first player to reach 3 points wins the game.

Good luck and have fun!
